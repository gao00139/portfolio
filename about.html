I'm Yunxia Gao, founder of Lens and Line, combining photography and design to create meaningful visual stories. With a passion for capturing authentic moments and building strong brand identities, I help individuals and businesses bring their vision to life.

About Statement

I am a passionate photographer and designer dedicated to creating visuals that evoke emotions and build connections. My work combines professionalism with warmth, ensuring that every photo and design piece reflects authenticity and modern aesthetics. Whether it's capturing family memories, elevating a real estate listing, or designing a brand's visual identity, I strive to deliver high-quality results that resonate with my clients.

Mission Statement

To capture timeless moments and craft visually compelling designs that tell authentic stories. Through professional photography and modern design, I help families, real estate professionals, and brands create visuals that are warm, engaging, and impactful.

Vision Statement

To be a trusted creative partner for families, real estate professionals, and brands, helping them tell their unique stories through modern and emotionally engaging photography and design. I aim to redefine the way people experience visual storytelling, ensuring that every image and design leaves a lasting impression.
